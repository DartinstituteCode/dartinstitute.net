What is Dart?

Dart Institute is a data analytics consulting company passionate about the architecture, development, and implementation of community data systems.

DATA ANALYTICS

Our data analytic and visualization experts help communities and businesses design systems that streamline data-informed decision making. We build dynamic dashboards to provide insightful reports of your data.

APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

Helping organizations and individuals meet their goal of attaining data-informed decision making is our main objective. Our team can assist your organization by finding meaning in the data. We're confident that we can help you accomplish your goals.

SURVEYS

Our data systems often use surveys to provide insightful data. We work hand-in-hand with our clients to develop surveys that aim to provide unbiased information.

Data

Analytics

Research
