Our team

Kevin Marble

Mr. Marble serves as the Director of Information Services for the State of Oklahoma for the past 8 years. Over his 19 years with ODMHSAS he has served in several positions including Senior software Developer and Application Development Manager. Mr. Marble has been in the IT field for almost 30 years with degrees in both hardware and software. Mr. Marble's team is responsible for all IT services for 12 Inpatient Hospitals, Crisis Centers and Community Mental Health Centers covering all 77 counties of Oklahoma. Mr. Marble's team provides services including Networking, Software Development, Helpdesk, Tele Medicine and Telecommunications services not only for ODMHSAS but they also are providing services to 8 of the largest agency's in Oklahoma State government including DHS and Health Department.



David Wright, Ph.D.

Dr. Wright serves as the Evaluation Projects Manager for the State of Oklahoma. The responsibilities of this position involve performing research, statistical analysis, and evaluation supporting treatment programs for justice-involved individuals, which in Oklahoma have an annual budget of over $40 million with almost $20 million designated for drug courts. Dr. Wright oversees the evaluation of Adult and Juvenile Drug Courts, DUI Courts, Veterans Treatment Courts, Mental Health Courts, Jail Diversion Programs, Prison Re-Entry Programs, and the Substance Abuse Mental Health Services Administration’(SAMHSA) Screening Brief Intervention Referral to Treatment Grant. Dr. Wright is also an Adjunct Professor for the University of Oklahoma and the University of Central Oklahoma. Dr. Wright also serves a consultant for JBS International in the areas of developing and evaluating performance and outcome measures related to SAMHSA grants. Prior to his current position, Dr. Wright was the Director of the Oklahoma Statistical Analysis Center and Director of Research at the Oklahoma Criminal Justice Resource Center. He has received numerous awards and honors for his research publications. Dr. Wright received his Ph.D. in political science from The University of Houston, after receiving his Master's degree at Oklahoma State University and his Bachelor's degree at Southwestern Oklahoma State University.



Austin Ralstin

Mr. Ralstin serves as the Senior System Health Analyst in Decision Support Services for the State of Oklahoma. In prior work, he served as the Project Evaluator on Oklahoma’s Screening Brief Intervention Referral to Treatment (SBIRT) Grant, assisted the Association of State and Criminal Investigation Agencies (ASCIA) in researching and writing model legislation for interstate law enforcement aid, and has served the State of Oklahoma as a legal intern. He also served as a statistic tutor and Graduate Assistant at the University of Central Oklahoma. Mr. Ralstin currently purses a Juris Doctorate specializing in Health and Public Law from the Oklahoma City University School of Law, after receiving a Master's degree in crime and intelligence analysis from the University of Central Oklahoma and a Bachelor's degree from Oklahoma University.
